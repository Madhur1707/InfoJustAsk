College Mapping

College mapping is a great counselling feature used by many educational counsellors. It allows students to explore college options based on their interests, academic abilities, and financial needs. College mapping takes the stress out of the decision-making process by allowing students to see the various options available to them.

Students can explore their options, compare different schools, and find the best fit for their needs. College mapping also helps students understand the admissions process and the prioritization of requirements needed to get accepted. With college mapping, students can find the right college and make an informed decision about where they should attend.

Key Features

Provides an overview of college options available, based on student’s qualifications and interests

Enables students to compare and contrast different colleges to determine which best fits their needs

Helps students identify scholarships and financial aid opportunities

Provides information about admissions requirements and deadlines

Offers guidance on creating and submitting college applications

Gives information about the academic and social environment at each college
