NEET UG

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (Undergraduate) or NEET (UG), formerly the All India Pre-Medical Test (AIPMT), is an all India pre-medical entrance test for students who wish to pursue undergraduate medical (MBBS), dental (BDS) and AYUSH (BAMS, BUMS, BHMS, etc.) courses in government and private institutions in India and also, for those intending to pursue primary medical qualification abroad.
The exam is conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA), which provides the results to the Directorate General of Health Services under Ministry of Health and Family Welfare and State Counselling Authorities for seat allocation.

NEET-UG is a single entrance test for admissions to MBBS and BDS colleges across India.
NEET UG is one of the largest exam in India in terms of registered applicants.
After the enactment of NMC Act 2019 in September 2019, NEET-UG became the sole entrance test for admissions to medical colleges in India including the All India Institutes of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) and Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education & Research (JIPMER) which until then conducted separate exams.

COURSES AFTER NEET UG

MBBS

MBBS, also known as Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery, is considered as the toughest examination and dominating professional course in India.

Just Ask provide direct admission in MBBS through Management and NRI quota. We provide admission to private medical colleges through NEET UG.

Eligibility Criteria

Biology Physics and Chemistry as main subjects in class 12.

Aspirant should be 17 years and not more than 25 years.

Candidate must have qualified NEET UG with good marks.

BDS

BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery) is a 5-years undergraduate program and the only approved professional dental course in India. It is a compulsory course for the students aspiring to work as dentists at government or private hospitals. BDS mainly focuses on training and introducing the students to the dental sciences and surgeries. The 5-year program involves 4 years of classroom education and 1 year of compulsory rotating internship.

The degree is regulated by the Dental Council of India (DCI). The various BDS subjects taught in the program include Dental Histology, Oral Pathology, Oral Surgery, etc. It is necessary for the students to register themselves in the Dental Council of India which regulates the BDS course so that they become eligible to practice their profession after the completion of their degree.

BAMS

Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) is a Bachelor’s Degree Course offered in India. It is a full-time 4+ years + 1-year internship course on Ayurveda, the holistic and ancient branch of medicine that focuses on preventive and curative health care. Ayurveda involves nutrition and lifestyle, herbal therapy, surgery and yoga. A BAMS degree course will teach you how to diagnose and cure people of various diseases. It will equip you with the knowledge of herbs, nutrition and diet, and of surgery and yoga. You will learn the study of body, mind and soul and how to use it for the benefit of humankind. A BAMS supplement modern medical education.

The objectives of the BAMS are:-

The goal of the Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) course is to provide the candidate with complete and comprehensive instruction in many elements of Ayurveda.


To introduce the student to the basic Ayurvedic principles and techniques of understanding the body, its functions, maintenance and wellbeing.


Based on the clinical evaluation and suitably identified and executed investigations, be able to diagnose and control acute and chronic disorders.



BSC NURSING

B.Sc Nursing or Bachelor of Science in Nursing is a 4-year undergraduate program that prepares students to serve humanity with the help of medical treatment. Students can pursue this course after completing their 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (as compulsory subjects). BSc Nursing course can also be studied by General Nursing and Midwifery (G.N.M) degree holders.

A B.Sc Nursing course focuses on imparting knowledge to students on critical care, and advanced analytical skills, and helps them inculcate values that are necessary for becoming professional nurses as per the National Health Policy 2002. This program is designed to help students address the healthcare sector’s needs of the nation, society, and individuals.

Admission to a BSc Nursing Course is provided through the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET). However, some states also conduct their own nursing entrance exam or have their own admission process by filling out online application forms. There is a chance of creating a bright future and promising career by studying BSc Nursing such as becoming a clinical nurse specialist, certified nurse midwife, case manager, nurse anaesthetist, nurse educator, nurse practitioner, or staff nurse.


NEET PG

NEET PG is a computer-based entrance test for admissions to MD/MS and PG diploma courses which are offered in various government, private, Deemed/Central, ESIC and AFMS medical institutions all over India. The students can get admissions in medical colleges through NEET. This exam is conducted nationwide once a year in online mode by the National Board of Education (NBE) for admissions to various medical colleges across the nation.

. All the medical graduates who want to go for PG courses from the best medical institutions should be aware of this exam. All the MBBS graduates are required to get themselves registered on the official website of NBE so that they can appear for the national level entrance exam.

The eligibility criteria for the exam are mentioned below:

Candidates must have completed MBBS from a university/institution affiliated by the MCI.


Must have completed the mandatory internship before a required time frame.


Must have been provisionally/permanently certified by the Medical Council of India or State Council of India.
